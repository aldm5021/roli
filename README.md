## What Makes ROLI Different?

Most MIDI controllers are just... controllers. You press a key, it sends a signal, done. But ROLI instruments track movement in multiple dimensions â€“ they call it "5D Touch." You can:

- **Press** (like normal velocity)
- **Slide** (left to right for pitch bends)
- **Glide** (between notes smoothly)
- **Lift** (how you release the key)
- **Strike** (initial attack)

It's basically the difference between typing an email and finger-painting. Both get the job done, but one's a lot more expressive.

Four DNA connectors mean you can build it out even more than the Seaboard M. Battery life is 6 hours. It's perfect for producers who want MPE expression but prefer traditional key feel.

### The Secret Weapon: Airwave

This thing is wild. It's a hand-tracking camera system that sits above your keyboard and watches your hands in 3D space. You can control sound with gestures in the air. Wave your hand for a crescendo. Flick your wrist to add layers. It works with any MIDI keyboard (after calibration), but it's designed for ROLI instruments.

For creators, it adds five more dimensions of expression. For learners, it tracks your finger positioning and technique, giving real-time feedback.
